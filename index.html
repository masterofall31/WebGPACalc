RRISD GPA Calculator

Round Rock ISD GPA is calculated on a semester rather than a year basis.

How to calculate semester grades: Combine both of the marking period grades and divide them by 2. Ex: MP1: 94, MP2: 96. (94+ 96) / 2 = 95 semester average for that class.

If the grade ends with a .5, round up to the next whole number.
Ex: MP1: 93 MP2: 96. (93 + 96) / 2 = 94.5, which rounds up to a 95 for the semester average grade for that class.

A weighted class at RRISD is usually a core or language class, however, check your school's course list to see what classes qualify as "weighted".

Instructions

Choose one of the options above for the type of GPA you want to calculate.

The non-cumulative GPA options are for calculating your GPA with the grades you have currently or for the current semester.

The cumulative GPA requires you to enter your cumulative GPA from your transcript to display your GPA with the current grades you have.
